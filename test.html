Automatic Room Heater

For my indoor hydroponics system I was running into some issues with the temperature. My hydroponics system is setup in a room in the basement of my house, as a result it can get quite cold down there if no extra heating is used. If the room temperature drops below 18°C plant growth will slow down significantly. From what I understand the ideal temperature range for plant growth is 22°C to 26°C so I built this device to keep the room within that range. The device takes temperature readings from a DHT11 temperature sensor, outputs the reading in celsius to two seven segment displays and if the temperture is below the lower threshold it will turn on a space heater until the upper threshold is reached.
