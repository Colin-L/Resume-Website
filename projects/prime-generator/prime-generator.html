Amazon Prime Generator

A few years ago amazon came out with their Amazon Prime subscription service offering free two day shipping. Along with it they released a free 30 day Amazon trial program were new users could take advantage of all the features amazon prime offers for free for 30 days to encourage people to pay for the service after their trial period ended.

It bacme apparent that this program could be exploited by making a new account every month and signing up for their free trial. This works so well because amazon doesn't do any email verification so any string that follows the format of an email would be enough to create an account (eg. Asdf@foo.ca would be valid).

For a few years before I really learned how to program I would manually make a new account every month and cancel the trial so I did not get charged at the end of it. Then in 2016 I discovered selenium py a tool that allows you to control a Firefox browser using Python code. With selenium py I was able to write a program that would automate the account creation process for me, saving me about 5 minutes of manual work per account.
